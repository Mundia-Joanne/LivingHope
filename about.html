Mission Statement

Living Hope Inc is a nonprofit that focuses primarily on reaching teenagers and young adults specifically in East Africa or other locations through mentorship and discipleship to raise up lifelong followers of Jesus.

Vision Statement

We are committed to helping young people develop wholistically by offering programs, camps, seminars, trainings, Bible studies, and conferences globally, and also focusing on developing relationships that nurture the whole person – physically, mentally, emotionally, socially and spiritually.
